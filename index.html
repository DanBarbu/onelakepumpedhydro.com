Energy Storage Rights

A revolutionary new take on energy storage. OLPHES — a single‑reservoir, modular and scalable pumped‑hydro technology — can be installed in gulfs, mining pit lakes, reservoirs and off‑shore, delivering storage with low deployment costs.

How It Works

The basic principle is the same as pumped‑hydro energy storage. The key difference is reservoir arrangement: OLPHES uses a smaller buoyant platform floating within a larger reservoir. Water is moved between them via floating pumps and turbines.

Potential

On lakes, cooling ponds, wastewater ponds or farm reservoirs, OLPHES paired with floating solar converts them into renewable energy assets with storage — reducing evaporation and improving water quality by aeration.
